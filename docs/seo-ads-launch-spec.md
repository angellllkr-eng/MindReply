## Positioning Rule

Use premium authority language without unverifiable claims. Say:
- "Built for global high-trust professional communication."
- "Worldwide executive communication intelligence."
- "Designed for UK, US, Canadian, Australian, European, and international professional teams."

Do not claim:
- "No. 1 worldwide" unless a third-party ranking proves it.

## Immediate Launch Steps

1. Confirm tracking env vars in Vercel.
2. Confirm `/solutions/*` pages fire `solution_landing_conversion_intent`.
3. Launch UK campaigns first with low daily budgets.
4. Use exact-match and phrase-match ad groups before broad match.
5. Exclude medical diagnosis, emergency, crisis, and regulated-advice keywords.
6. Pause any campaign where checkout tracking is not verified.
7. Review spend, CTR, conversion intent, checkout starts, and paid conversions daily.
